## How to Formulate a Positioning Statement

For [who], [product] is the [category] that [benefit], unlike [alternative], because [reason to believe]. (Geoffrey Moore)

## Who

What do we know about our targeted prosumers? Understanding the audience for your solution is the most critical decision, do not rush making your decision on this and make sure that every member of your team understands and agrees with "who" the solution is for because it is the only way they can do effective work.

For Open Ecological Technology, the "who" are technologists that believe sharing knowledge and decentralizing production has the power to create a more resilient world.

Where do you fit in amongst available solutions to the problem you solve? Consider possible categories your solution could belong to - and then, define your own category.
